About this website

Hi there! My name is Tina Ma and I am an aspiring concept artist. "Mute" is my biggest project thus far; a conceptual game design that aims to illustrate social anxiety through an interactive lens. I've collaborated with my friend Josephine Chen to bring you the "Mute" website, a place for you to explore the game concept, find helplines or learn more about the issue of social anxiety.

If you like my work here, please check out more of my stuff using my social media links!
