F A Q

  • What is Mute?

    Mute is a concept for an adventure story game created by Tina Ma. It focuses on the theme of social anxiety and its goal is to raise awareness of this issue in a more fun and interactive way.
  • When can I play this game?

    Mute is still in its conceptual stage and Tina is still developing this concept. We're sorry that it probably won't be anytime soon, but if you're interested in seeing other stuff Tina has worked on or want to support what she does, check out her social media links on the home page!
  • Why social anxiety?

    Social anxiety is an issue Tina feels isn't talked about enough. The resources available aren't too interesting either, most of them being quite heavy on text. Tina hopes that her game can bring a new perspective to mental illnesses, making it easier for people to talk about.
